A practice across land, language, and form.

Select a category to open its work — photography from remote field sites, writing, conceptual projects, installation, and applications.

To luxuriate in discomfort is to step into challenging conditions and stay there long enough for perception to change — driven by adventure, rewarded with discovery.

Lisa Wood works at remote field sites across volcanic, desert, winter, and ice terrain, returning with large-scale photographs — and with writing and installation — that ask for the same sustained attention they were made with.
